Membership

Best Kids Play Zone in Bangalore

We want you to feel like you are getting value for money, so if you are going to be visiting Tumble Time Play Area more times in a month or year, you are better off under a indoor play area membership. Indoor Play Area Membership aims to reward frequent visitors. It reduces the cost of entry for each child with unlimited hours and makes the accompanying adults free (max 2 adults) if they are not using the play zones. Due to the fact that we have a limit on the number of visitors we can have at one time, indoor play area membership does not provide for unlimited free entry for children.


Whilst we ask that a indoor play area membership be filled out in the name of an adult, it is attached to the child/ren named on the indoor play area membership form. As a result, different carers can bring the child under the membership. This is great for nannies/grand parents and its great to see our customers using their memberships frequently.


Please Note: You cannot substitute children under the membership. For example, if you buy a 1-child membership it is only in the name of the child listed on the membership form. If you list more than one child on the membership form, we will apply the membership only to the first child listed on the membership form.
